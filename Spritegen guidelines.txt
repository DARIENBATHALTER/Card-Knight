Card Knight: Astral Arcana - Asset List & Sprite Descriptors
Global Style Descriptor

Pixel art fantasy JRPG asset, early 2000s handheld action RPG proportions, inspired by Game Boy Advance-era isometric RPGs and high-resolution PSP-era Final Fantasy spritework. Clean readable silhouette, detailed but not noisy, warm painterly pixel shading, deep navy and antique gold accents, subtle magical glow effects, transparent background, no drop shadow unless specified, ready for 640x360 fantasy RPG game.

For overworld assets:

Flat isometric fantasy pixel art, top-down three-quarter perspective, consistent with a 640x360 indie RPG, painterly pixel texture, clean outline, transparent background, usable as modular map asset.

For battle assets:

Side-view tactical grid battle pixel art, fantasy card-magic JRPG style, ornate dark-blue and gold UI language, readable at small scale, dramatic magical VFX, transparent background unless background is requested.

1. Main Character: Oden
Oden - Core Visual Identity

Oden is a young wandering Card Knight with sandy-blond spiky hair, blue eyes, youthful determined expression, deep navy scarf-cape with subtle gold arcane trim, leather traveler armor, brass/gold pauldrons and bracers, dark blue trousers, brown boots, red cloth tabs at the waist, sword sheathed across his back or hip, small card pouch or deck case at his belt. He looks like a fantasy JRPG protagonist, agile and heroic rather than bulky, with early 2000s Square Enix energy but original design.

Key identifiers:

Sandy-blond spiky hair
Blue scarf-cape with gold trim
Leather and light plate armor
Red waist cloth
Brass/gold details
Sword
Card Knight / magical duelist energy
Oden - Overworld Idle Sprite, 8 Directions

Oden overworld idle sprite sheet, humanoid proportions, not chibi, not squat, similar to Boktai / Mega Man Battle Network / Kingdom Hearts Chain of Memories overworld proportions. Eight static poses: up, down, left, right, up-left, up-right, down-left, down-right. Transparent background. Each sprite evenly spaced and labeled. Cape hangs naturally and shifts according to facing direction. Sword visible on back where appropriate.

Oden - Run Animation, Cardinal Directions

Oden four-frame running animation sprite sheet, directions up, down, left, right. Humanoid proportions, readable legs and alternating feet, cape flapping behind him like Solar Boy Django/Boktai. Each row contains one direction, four frames per row. Transparent background. Consistent scale, consistent outfit, consistent hair silhouette.

Oden - Run Animation, Up Diagonals

Oden four-frame running animation sprite sheet for diagonal movement up-left and up-right. Character should be visibly running away from camera at a diagonal, showing mostly back and side profile. Feet alternate clearly: frame 1 left foot forward, frame 2 passing pose, frame 3 right foot forward, frame 4 passing pose. Cape streams backward and slightly outward. Two rows: up-left and up-right, four frames each. Transparent background.

Oden - Run Animation, Down Diagonals

Oden four-frame running animation sprite sheet for diagonal movement down-left and down-right. Character should be visibly running toward camera at a diagonal, showing face and torso at three-quarter angle. Feet alternate clearly: frame 1 left foot forward, frame 2 passing pose, frame 3 right foot forward, frame 4 passing pose. Cape trails behind in opposite direction of travel. Two rows: down-left and down-right, four frames each. Transparent background.

Oden - Battle Ready Pose

Oden battle stance sprite, facing right, both feet planted, wide grounded stance, fists ready or one hand near card/deck, sword visible but not drawn unless specified. Blue scarf-cape flowing behind him to the left, blond spiky hair, focused expression, light armor and leather gear. Larger battle sprite scale, detailed pixel art, transparent background.

Oden - Card Casting Pose

Oden battle casting sprite, facing right, one hand extended forward holding a glowing arcane card, other arm back for balance, feet planted on magical circle, blue scarf-cape swept behind him, focused expression. Transparent background, optional blue-white magical aura around card.

Oden battle sprite, facing right, mid-slash with short sword or longsword, blue crescent slash effect, dynamic forward lean, cape trailing, feet grounded. Transparent background.

16. Suggested First Production Batch

For the demo, I’d prioritize these in this order:

Oden overworld idle - 8 directions
Oden run - 8 directions, 4 frames each
Oden battle idle, cast, slash, card throw, hit, victory
Flat grassland terrain set
Path tiles and transition tiles
Core environmental object sheet
Battle grid tiles
Slime, goblin mage, wolf, pumpkin imp
Core cards: Sword Slash, Ranged, Ignis, Aqua, Terra, Aero, Fulgis, Glacis, Cure Leaf
Core VFX: fire, lightning, ice, wind, healing, hit spark
Title screen card and UI panels
One summon: Astral Aegis

That would be enough to build a convincing vertical slice: overworld movement, one route, one dungeon room, one battle, card selection, elemental effects, and a dramatic summon payoff.
